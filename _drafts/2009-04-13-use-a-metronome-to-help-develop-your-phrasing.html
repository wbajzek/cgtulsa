--- layout: post title: "Use a metronome to help develop your phrasing" date: 2009-04-13T12:44:00-05:00 ---
* set metronome to short time values to help solve technical problems
* learn to play a phrase as one gesture rather than a series of notes/events
* practice one-octave scales with metronome clicking on first note and the octave
* set it to one click per bar or longer if possible to develop phrasing
